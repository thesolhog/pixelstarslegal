# Privacy Policy

**Pixel Stars: Arcade Hockey** — Effective May 2026

## Overview

Pixel Stars: Arcade Hockey is a single-player arcade hockey game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect (very little) and how it is used.

## No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not require an account, login, email address, or any form of registration. All game progress is stored locally on your device.

## No Social Features

The app contains no social features such as chat, messaging, friend lists, or user-generated content. Leaderboards, if present, use anonymous identifiers only.

## Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as:

- App opens and session duration
- General gameplay events (matches played, features used)
- Device type and operating system version
- Crash reports

This data is aggregated and anonymous. It cannot be used to identify individual users. It is used solely to improve the game experience.

## In-App Purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect, store, or have access to any payment information, credit card numbers, or billing details.

## Advertising

This app does not display advertisements.

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Pixel Stars: Arcade Hockey is designed to be safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since we collect no personal data from any user, the app is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

## Data Storage

All game save data, settings, and progress are stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store user data. If you uninstall the app, all local data is removed.

## Third-Party Services

- **Firebase Analytics (Google)** — anonymous usage analytics. [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- **Apple App Store / Google Play** — in-app purchase processing. Subject to their respective privacy policies.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
